## Example Sentences

Japanese, English, and Chinese example sentences are primarily matched from Tatoeba text exports and then selected for JLPT vocabulary practice. Tatoeba textual sentences are generally licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 2.0 France (CC BY 2.0 FR), unless an individual sentence has another license on Tatoeba. Reuse, modification, and distribution require attribution to the sentence author where available.

- Tatoeba downloads: https://tatoeba.org/en/downloads
- Tatoeba terms of use: https://tatoeba.org/en/terms_of_use
- CC BY 2.0 FR: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.0/fr/

This project does not use Tatoeba audio. Some Chinese translations that were missing from Tatoeba were generated locally by RIKKA with Ollama/Qwen from the Tatoeba Japanese/English source sentence. These generated translations should still preserve attribution to the underlying Tatoeba sentence.

Per-sentence attribution data is stored in:

- `hvpt_site/data/tatoeba-sentence-attribution.json`

The attribution file includes Tatoeba sentence IDs, sentence page URLs, and available contributor names. Sentence pages follow this URL format:

- `https://tatoeba.org/en/sentences/show/<sentenceId>`
